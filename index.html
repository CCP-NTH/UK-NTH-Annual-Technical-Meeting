About the meeting

Overview

The UK NTH Annual Technical Meeting brings together researchers, industry practitioners, government and funding representatives, and the wider nuclear thermal-hydraulics community.

The meeting provides an opportunity to share recent technical progress, discuss UK capability needs, and identify future opportunities in fission, fusion, high-fidelity simulation, experiments, data-driven modelling and software development.

Before you attend

Conference Notes

  • This is a draft programme and may be subject to minor changes.
  • Most speaking slots are 20 minutes including questions. Speakers should aim to present for around 15 minutes, leaving 5 minutes for questions and transition.
  • Flash talks are 5 minutes.
  • The conference dinner will not include alcoholic beverages funded by CCP-NTH/STFC/EPSRC. Attendees may purchase their own drinks if they wish.
  • Group photo participation is only for attendees who have given photo permission.
