About the Meeting

The Annual Technical Meeting provides a forum for researchers and practitioners in nuclear thermal hydraulics to present recent advances, exchange ideas, and discuss emerging challenges.

The meeting will feature technical presentations, flash talks, and opportunities for discussion across academia and industry. Participation from early-career researchers is particularly encouraged.
